Options become a detection inspector

The form is no longer only about configuration. It becomes a place to understand why TopSkip decided something was promotional, which model was used, and what fallback logic applied.

Why it differs

Detection is represented spatially, not as plain text status. This changes the dominant mental model from switch-first to inspect-first.

Who it serves

Users who want confidence, evidence, and clarity about false positives or misses.
